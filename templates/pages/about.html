Meet the Developer

Ajmal U K & UTHAKKAN

Hello! I'm Ajmal U K, the solo developer behind UTHAKKAN, an indie web development studio based in Kannur, Kerala, India.

ZyRace was born out of a passion for physics-based games and a desire to create something fun, accessible, and free for everyone. What started as a small experiment with Matter.js has grown into a full-fledged racing adventure played by thousands.

My mission is simple: Build cool stuff that people love. Whether it's a resume builder to help people land their dream jobs or a racing game to help them relax, I pour my heart into every line of code.

Other Projects

Beyond gaming, I specialize in building useful web tools and applications:

  • Resume Builder: A tool to create professional resumes in minutes.
  • Web Utilities: Various open-source tools for developers.
  • Mobile Apps: Cross-platform experiences for productivity.
