Support Topics

Troubleshooting

  • Game not loading? Try clearing your browser cache.
  • Performance issues? Close other tabs to free up memory.
  • No sound? Check your browser tab isn't muted.
  • Progress lost? Ensure cookies/local storage are enabled.

Controls

  • Desktop: Arrow keys or WASD to drive and balance.
  • Mobile: On-screen pedals for gas/brake, tilt buttons for balance.
  • Reset: Press 'R' or the reset button if you get stuck.
  • Pause: Press 'P' or the pause icon.

Gameplay

  • Unlock new parts by completing levels.
  • Earn stars for faster times and stunts.
  • Experiment with different builds for each track.
  • Watch replays to learn from your mistakes.
