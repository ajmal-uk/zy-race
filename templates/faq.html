Frequently Asked Questions

What makes ZyRace special in car games?
ZyRace combines car tuning, realistic physics puzzles, and racing adventures in a colorful world of bridges, mountains, and water hazards. It's free, fun, and full of surprises for online car enthusiasts!
Is ZyRace completely free with no ads?
Yes! Enjoy all levels, cars, and features without ads or payments. Pure adventure at zero cost in this browser-based racing game.
How do I tune my first car in the vehicle tuner?
Start the game, select parts from the garage, and snap them together. Test on the track and tweak for the perfect ride in physics racing!
Does ZyRace work on mobile for on-the-go racing?
Totally! Optimized touch controls let you tune and race anywhere, anytime in this no-download car simulator.
Who built ZyRace and why focus on physics puzzles?
Indie dev Ajmal U K from Kerala, India, through UTHAKKAN. The realistic physics add depth to car challenges — follow @ajmal_uk_ for tips and updates!
How can I get more tips or report issues?
Follow @ajmal_uk_ on Twitter for updates and tips. For issues, email contact@uthakkan@gmail.com. We're always improving ZyRace!
Are there plans for new features or updates?
Yes! More tracks, cars, and tuning options are in the works. Stay tuned via the blog or social media for announcements.
