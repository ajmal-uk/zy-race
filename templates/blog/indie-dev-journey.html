The Indie Journey

Building dreams one line of code at a time.

Ajmal U K  |  November 2025  |  Story

Hi everyone! I'm Ajmal U K, the developer behind ZyRace. I run a small indie studio called UTHAKKAN from the beautiful coastal town of Kannur in Kerala, India.

The Spark

I've always been fascinated by how things work. Whether it was taking apart toy cars as a kid or debugging complex algorithms now, the curiosity has always been there. ZyRace started as a weekend project. I wanted to see if I could build a car game that ran entirely in the browser without needing heavy downloads.

"The best games aren't always the ones with the biggest budgets. They're the ones made with the most heart."

The Challenges of Solo Dev

Being a solo developer means wearing many hats. One minute I'm the lead engineer fixing a physics bug, the next I'm the UI designer picking color palettes, and then I'm the marketing team writing these blog posts!

It's exhausting, but incredibly fulfilling. Every pixel on the screen is a decision I made. Every feature is something I built because I thought it would be fun.

Why "UTHAKKAN"?

UTHAKKAN represents my identity and my roots. It's a reminder to stay grounded while aiming high. Through UTHAKKAN, I aim to build not just games, but useful tools like resume builders and productivity apps that help people in their daily lives.

What's Next?

ZyRace is just the beginning. I have plans for more tracks, more cars, and maybe even a multiplayer mode. But beyond that, I want to keep learning and sharing my journey with you all.

Thank you for playing, for reading, and for being part of this adventure.
