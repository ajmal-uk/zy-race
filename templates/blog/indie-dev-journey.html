Ajmal U K  |   November 2025  |   Founder Story

My name is Ajmal U K, and I am the founder of UTHAKKAN. I come from Kannur, a small coastal town in Kerala, India. I don't have a massive team, millions in VC funding, or a fancy office in Silicon Valley. What I have is a laptop, an internet connection, and an obsession with code.

The "Zero Budget" Philosophy

In the world of indie game development, constraints are your best friend. When I decided to build ZyRace, I set one rule: It must be free to build and free to play.

This philosophy forced me to innovate. Instead of buying expensive assets, I learned to generate textures procedurally. Instead of paying for heavy server hosting, I optimized the netcode to run efficiently on lightweight instances. This journey taught me that resourcefulness matters more than resources.

"You don't need permission to build something great. You just need to start typing."

The Tech Stack: Why Python & Vanilla JS?

Many developers asked why I didn't use Unity or Unreal Engine. The answer is simple: Control.

By building the engine from scratch using Python (Flask) for the backend and Vanilla JavaScript for the frontend, I gained a deep understanding of every byte that goes over the wire. This allows ZyRace to load in under 2 seconds on 3G networks—something bloated game engines struggle to achieve.

Late Nights in Kannur

The journey wasn't easy. There were nights when the physics engine would break, sending cars flying into the stratosphere (a bug we later turned into a "feature"). There were times managing college studies alongside full-stack development felt impossible.

But then, the first user review came in: "This game runs so smoothly on my old phone. Thank you." That one sentence made every sleepless night worth it.

The Vision of UTHAKKAN

UTHAKKAN is more than just a company name; it's a statement. It stands for independent thinking and resilient building. Moving forward, my goal is to expand UTHAKKAN into a hub for:

  • Open Source Tools: Releasing the utilities I built for ZyRace to the community.
  • AI Integration: Using LLMs to create dynamic game narratives.
  • Education: Helping aspiring developers in India build world-class software from their bedrooms.

Join the Adventure

ZyRace is just the start. If you're a developer, a gamer, or a dreamer, I invite you to follow this journey. Let's build the future of the web, one line of code at a time.
