ZyRace Garage

Choose your ride wisely.

ZyRace Team  |  December 2025  |  Game Guide

In ZyRace, your vehicle isn't just a skin—it's a physics object with unique mass, suspension, and friction properties. Choosing the right car for the right track is half the battle. Here is the official guide to the ZyRace garage.

1. The Scout (Starter Jeep)

The jack-of-all-trades. The Scout is balanced, reliable, and free. It has decent climbing ability but struggles on extreme vertical walls.

Speed: ⭐⭐⭐
Grip: ⭐⭐⭐
Stability: ⭐⭐⭐⭐
Cost: Free

2. The Apex (Sports Car)

Speed is its middle name. The Apex sits low to the ground, giving it great aerodynamics. However, its low suspension makes it vulnerable to bumpy terrains where the chassis might scrape the ground.

Speed: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
Grip: ⭐⭐
Stability: ⭐⭐
Cost: 1000 Coins

3. The Titan (Monster Truck)

When in doubt, crush it. The Titan features massive wheels and soft suspension. It can drive over almost any obstacle seamlessly but suffers from a high center of gravity, risking rollovers.

Speed: ⭐⭐⭐
Grip: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
Stability: ⭐⭐⭐
Cost: 2500 Coins

How to Earn Coins Fast

Want that Titan? Here are tips to farm coins:

  • Air Time: You earn bonus coins for every second spent in the air.
  • Flips: Safely landing a front-flip or back-flip awards 500 coins instantly.
  • Daily multiplier: Play every day to increase your coin multiplier upto 2x.
