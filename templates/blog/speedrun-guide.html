Speedrun Guide

Seconds matter. Milliseconds count.

ZyRace Team  |  December 2025  |  Advanced Guide

Speedrunning is the art of completing a game as fast as possible. In ZyRace, where physics is king, speedrunning requires a deep understanding of momentum, friction, and even glitches. Here is how to shave seconds off your time.

1. The "Sonic Start"

Don't just hold the gas when the countdown ends. Tap the gas rapidly during the "3... 2... 1..." count. If timed correctly, you'll start with higher RPM, launching you forward instantly.

2. Optimal Air Rotation

Air resistance in ZyRace is minimal, but landing angle is everything.

Pro Tip: Always aim to land with your rear wheels first on flat ground, but land with all 4 wheels simultaneously on slopes.

3. Corner Cutting with Wall Riding

Some levels have vertical walls. Instead of slowing down, accelerate into them. If you have enough speed, the centrifugal force will keep you stuck to the wall/ceiling, allowing you to bypass entire sections of the track.

4. Death Abuse

Sounds scary, but it's a valid tactic. In some long levels, checkpoints are placed after difficult obstacles. If you are low on health or fuel, intentionally crashing right after hitting a checkpoint respawns you with full stats, saving time on finding pickups.

5. Frame Rules

ZyRace runs at 60Hz. Input is processed every frame. Tapping a key for 2 frames vs 5 frames makes a huge difference in mid-air rotation. Practice tapping lightly.
