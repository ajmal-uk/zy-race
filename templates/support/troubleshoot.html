Common Issues & Fixes

Game Not Loading

Is the game stuck on the loading screen or showing a blank page?

Fix: Clear your browser cache and cookies. Ensure JavaScript is enabled. Try disabling ad-blockers as they might interfere with game scripts.

Controls Not Working

Car not moving or responding to keys?

Fix: Click on the game window to ensure it has focus. Check if your keyboard is working properly. On mobile, ensure you are in landscape mode.

Physics Glitches

Car falling through the ground or flying into space?

Fix: This can happen on slower devices. Try closing other tabs to free up resources. Refresh the page to reset the physics engine.

Slow Performance

Low FPS or stuttering gameplay?

Fix: ZyRace uses WebGL. Ensure hardware acceleration is enabled in your browser settings. Update your graphics drivers.

Progress Not Saving

Lost your unlocked levels or car parts?

Fix: ZyRace uses local storage. Ensure your browser is not set to "Clear data on exit". Do not play in Incognito/Private mode if you want to save progress.
