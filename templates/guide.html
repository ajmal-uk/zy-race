How to Play ZyRace

1. Getting Started

Welcome to ZyRace! Your goal is to build a vehicle capable of traversing difficult terrain, collecting stars, and reaching the finish line without crashing.

When you first start, you'll have a basic chassis. It's up to you to add wheels, engines, and other parts to make it move.

2. The Garage & Tuning

The Garage is where the magic happens. Here you can customize your vehicle to suit the specific challenges of each track.

  • Chassis: The core of your car. Different shapes offer different balance points.
  • Wheels: Choose between grip, speed, or size. Big wheels help with rough terrain, while smaller ones accelerate faster.
  • Engines: More power means more speed, but also more weight. Balance is key.
  • Suspension: Critical for landing jumps and keeping your wheels on the ground.

Simply drag and drop parts onto your chassis. Experiment with different combinations!

3. Racing & Physics

ZyRace uses a realistic physics engine. This means momentum, gravity, and friction all play a huge role.

  • Throttle Control: Don't just hold down the gas. Feather the throttle to maintain traction.
  • Balance: Use the arrow keys (or tilt on mobile) to lean your car forward or backward in the air. This is essential for landing safely.
  • Momentum: Build up speed before big hills. Use the downhill sections to gain free speed.

4. Tips for Success

  • If you keep flipping over, try lowering your center of gravity by moving heavy parts lower.
  • If you get stuck on a steep hill, try a more powerful engine or grippier tires.
  • Watch out for bridges! They can be bouncy. Approach them with a steady speed.
  • Collect stars to unlock new parts and levels.
