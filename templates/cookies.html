ZyRace Cookies: Fueling Your Gaming Adventure Responsibly

At ZyRace, we use cookies to enhance your free online monster truck experience. This policy explains what cookies we use, why, and how you can manage them. Last updated: November 20, 2025.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our site. They help remember preferences, track usage, and ensure smooth browser-based racing without downloads.

Essential Cookies

These are required for basic functionality, like session management during truck building and physics puzzles. They don't track you across sites.

Analytics Cookies

Optional cookies help us understand how players interact with tracks and features. We use anonymized data to improve ZyRace adventures.

No Marketing Cookies

ZyRace is ad-free! We don't use cookies for targeted ads or third-party marketing, keeping your focus on epic races.

Cookies We Use

Cookie Name Type Purpose Duration
session_id Essential Maintains user session for building and racing Session
_ga Analytics (Google Analytics) Tracks site usage anonymously 2 years
_gid Analytics (Google Analytics) Short-term site analytics 24 hours
consent_cookie Essential Stores cookie consent preferences 1 year

Managing Your Cookies

You can manage cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers let you block or delete them. Note: Blocking essential cookies may affect site functionality, like saving your monster truck builds.

  • Browser Settings: Check your browser's help section (e.g., Chrome, Firefox) for cookie controls.
  • Opt-Out: For analytics, visit Google Analytics Opt-Out.
