ZyRace Privacy Policy

At ZyRace, we believe in keeping things simple and private. As a free browser-based car racing game developed by UTHAKKAN, we designed it to be fun without invading your personal space. Below, we outline exactly what data we handle (or don't) and how we ensure your peace of mind.

Personal Information Collection

ZyRace does not collect personal information directly from players. The game is free to play and requires no account or registration. You can jump in, tune your car, and race without ever sharing your name, email, or any identifying details.

Usage of Third-Party Tools

The website may use Google Analytics to gather anonymous usage statistics. This helps us improve the game experience for everyone — like optimizing load times or popular tracks — without tracking individuals.

Data Collected by Google Analytics

Any data used by Google Analytics is strictly non-personal and includes:

  • Device type (e.g., mobile or desktop)
  • Browser type (e.g., Chrome, Firefox)
  • Country/region (general location, not precise)
  • Non-personal usage activity (e.g., pages visited, time spent)

No private information like names, emails, passwords, or payment details is collected. Ever.

Data Protection Practices

All data is handled in accordance with general data protection practices, including compliance with GDPR where applicable. We use secure, industry-standard methods to process any minimal anonymous data. Players can freely enjoy the game without worrying about personal data collection.

Your Rights and Choices

You have full control:

  • Opt-Out: Disable Google Analytics via your browser settings or extensions like uBlock Origin.
  • Access/Update: Since no personal data is collected, there's nothing to access or update.
  • Deletion: Clear your browser cache to reset any local game data (scores, progress).
  • Questions: For any concerns, reach out via contact@uthakkan@gmail.com.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally to reflect improvements. Check back here for the latest version. By playing ZyRace, you agree to these practices.

This policy was last updated on November 21, 2025. Developed with transparency by Ajmal U K at UTHAKKAN, Kannur, Kerala, India.
